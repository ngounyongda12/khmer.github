Border Tensions Continue Amid Fragile Ceasefire

The guns along the Thai-Cambodian border have been silent for three weeks, yet daily accusations continue between the two countries. Cambodia has been more media-savvy, while Thailand struggles to coordinate its narrative.

Mine Accusations

Thai soldiers demonstrating mine detection techniques

Thailand has accused Cambodia of laying new PMN-2 anti-personnel mines, causing injuries to Thai soldiers. Cambodia denies this, citing old mines from the 1980s.

Preah Vihear Temple Dispute

Preah Vihear temple allegedly damaged during clashes

The historical Preah Vihear temple is central to the border dispute. Cambodia claims Thai shelling caused damage, while Thailand denies the allegations.

Political and Social Impact

Border clashes and disputes have displaced thousands, disrupted trade, and worsened nationalist tensions. Many Cambodian migrant workers have left Thailand, affecting both economies.
